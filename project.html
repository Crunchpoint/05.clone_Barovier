Beyond the evanescent charm.

There is a close, inseparable bond between an interior design and its various elements. A chain that links design objects, selected from a myriad of items, and the thinking of those who have designed the space, for one specific client. Public or private, small or large places that all narrate a tangible sense of passion. 
 
Every project made with pieces by Barovier&Toso is unique: there are no standard solutions, only flexible products that can adapt to the needs of each designer. Finishes, colours, sizes and special application solutions are all parts of the company’s DNA. To meet the expectations of architects and designer is one of our top priorities.
